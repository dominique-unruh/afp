Abstract

In this work, we prove the lower bound ln(H_n) - ln(5/3) for the partial sum of the Prime Harmonic series and, based on this, the divergence of the Prime Harmonic Series ‚àë[p prime] ¬∑ 1/p.

The proof relies on the unique squarefree decomposition of natural numbers. This is similar to Euler's original proof (which was highly informal and morally questionable).

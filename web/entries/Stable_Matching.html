Stable Matching

Peter Gammie üåê

October 24, 2016

Abstract

We mechanize proofs of several results from the matching with contracts literature, which generalize those of the classical two-sided matching scenarios that go by the name of stable marriage. Our focus is on game theoretic issues. Along the way we develop executable algorithms for computing optimal stable matches.
